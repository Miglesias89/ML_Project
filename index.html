Predicting Teacher Turnover in Your Building

Principals: The purpose of this tool is to use known characteristics about teachers to predict the likelihood of these teachers leaving your school within the next year. This can help you prepare to intervene and retain teachers that you'd want to stay, or it can help you plan for the number of new teachers you are likely to need to hire in the upcoming year.
